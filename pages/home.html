About us

FacioQuo is an organization of experienced software creators that builds online tools, microsites, and other utilities.

Sometimes we're just goofing around. Other times we're building things for our professional lives. Most of the time, we're just trying to help people.

We're mostly known for our popular open-source financial market analysis and stock indicator software.

Everyone is welcome to contribute to our public projects.
